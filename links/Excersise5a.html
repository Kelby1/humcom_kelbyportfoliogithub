The board features pneumatic, 10-inch tires that grip well, offer stability and allow you to make turns without slowing down. It also features full suspension, so your ride down the fairway is smooth no matter how many undulations are in the layoff. The motor features a 4.3:1 gear ratio, allowing you to travel up to 13 mph, and the extended range battery won’t give out at any time you’re on the course. Learning to steer takes only a matter of minutes as you figure out how to balance properly. Once you have it, it becomes second nature. The LCD display lets you know how fast you’re moving, how much battery power remains and more. And when the round is done, the folding handle makes storage simple. You’ll be the envy of your playing partners with this Cycleboard Personal Golf Vehicle.
